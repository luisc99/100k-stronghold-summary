In 2024, I was watching one of Couriway's streams, and chat was talking about what sort of things might be in the seeds that get rolled, but never actually found during the run. Inspired by this, I wrote some (somewhat slow) code to scan through all 128 strongholds in all of Couriway's seeds, to see if there were any 8-eye portals. I didn't find any, but did find a bunch of other interesting things, and since then, I've continued to scan all of the seeds rolled throughout the 100K challenge, usually within a few minutes of the run being completed, and share the coolest finds with the stream. This site is intended to document all of the interesting things I find, to avoid them getting lost to time.

For those who are unaware, each world has 128 strongholds, which generate in ring around 0,0. As these rings grow, the number of strongholds in each one increases. Collectively, the 6th and 7th rings contain exactly half of a world's strongholds, which is why so many things on this page seem to be within those two rings. Less than 15% of strongholds are within the first three rings. It is somewhat rare Couri will travel far enough in the Nether to go beyond third ring, which is why he is unlikely to have come across most things on this page.

High Eye Counts

Every frame in an end portal has a 10% chance of generating with an Eye of Ender already placed. On average, a portal is most likely to generate with one eye pre-placed, with around 92% of portals having three eyes or fewer. The odds of finding a portal with a given eye count can be found in the Eye Summaries table at the top of this page.

Across the entire 100K challenge, it is likely the most eyes that will ever generate in a portal is 9, with a roughly 87% chance of one existing, but less than 2% chance of Couri visiting one. The most eyes that have generated so far is 7, which he has visited once in a run.

The odds of a 12-eye portal occurring in the entire 100K challenge is approximately 1 in 78,128, or in other words Couri would need to play almost 8 billion seeds. A search for 12-eye end portals found approximately 8.7 million seeds, however this search only included portals in the first stronghold ring. Despite common misconception, it is theoretically possible (albeit extremely unlikely) to randomly generate a world with a 12-eye portal without using a set seed.
